# F&O Content Performance & Growth Analysis – Trading with Groww

## Objective
To analyze the performance of Futures & Options (F&O) focused YouTube content published by *Trading with Groww* and identify key drivers of engagement, audience growth, and content effectiveness. The analysis aims to support data-driven content optimization aligned with Groww’s educational and growth objectives.

## Data Source
- YouTube Channel: Trading with Groww  
- Data extracted using a free YouTube channel video list extractor  
- Dataset includes video-level metrics such as views, likes, comments, duration, publishing date, and derived engagement rates

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets / Microsoft Excel  
- KPI-based performance analysis  
- Content segmentation and weekly trend analysis  

## Key Insights
- F&O videos focused on practical strategies, options concepts, and market explainers consistently outperform other content types in terms of engagement.
- Engagement Rate (%) provides a more reliable indicator of content effectiveness than views alone, highlighting strong audience resonance for educational formats.
- Weeks with consistent posting frequency show higher cumulative views and engagement growth compared to irregular publishing periods.
- Short-to-medium duration videos demonstrate higher engagement efficiency, indicating user preference for concise, high-value explanations.
- A small set of recurring content themes contributes disproportionately to total engagement, suggesting repeat viewership and content loyalty.

## Strategic Recommendations
- Increase focus on high-performing F&O topics such as options strategies, risk management, and concept-driven explainers.
- Maintain a consistent weekly publishing schedule to improve algorithmic visibility and sustained user growth.
- Track engagement rate as a primary KPI alongside views to better evaluate content performance.
- Align video duration with observed high-engagement ranges while reserving longer formats for proven, high-demand topics.
- Develop repeatable content series around top-performing themes to strengthen audience retention and brand trust.

## Business Impact
Implementing these recommendations can improve content reach, viewer retention, and engagement quality. A data-driven F&O content strategy strengthens Groww’s position as a trusted financial education platform and supports scalable user growth through informed content planning.
